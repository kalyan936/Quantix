Quantix Innovations was founded in 2022 by a small team of data scientists with a mission to make AI adoption practical and accessible for enterprises.

Starting with pilot projects in India, we quickly grew to support clients in multiple industries, helping enterprises in retail and healthcare adopt AI to solve critical challenges and drive measurable business outcomes.
